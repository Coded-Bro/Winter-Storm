About Winter Storm

6% buy tax & 6% sell tax.

For Holders

WINTER STORM incentivises holders to buy and hold the $STORM token! How? By utilising an auto-reward mechanism built into the STORM Smart Contract. This awesome feature redistributes a 2% transaction tax taken from every buy and sell, back to the remaining holders of the $STORM token in the form of Pepe coin ($Pepe). We will also make available the STORM holders Rewards Dashboard Tracker which allows $STORM holders to view their rewards received and current $STORM balance.

For Stakers

WINTER STORM incentivises stakers to buy and stake the $STORM token! How? By utilising an auto-reward mechanism built into the STORM Bonus Pool Smart Contract. This awesome feature distributes rewards according to the amount of $STORM tokens staked, and duration of time of stake to the stakers of the $STORM token in the form of $STORM ($STM). We will also make available the STORM stakers staking Dashboard which allows $STORM stakers to stake $STORM, view their rewards received, and current $STORM balance.
